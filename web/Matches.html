Six Inventational

The Six Invitational 2018 is the first major Rainbow Six tournament of 2018 and the second iteration of the Six Invitational, featuring 16 teams from across the world fighting for a share of the $500,000 USD prize pool in Montreal. The Six Invitational 2018 also marks the end of Year 2 of the Rainbow Six competitive season, with Year 3 being announced during the Playoffs of the event.
